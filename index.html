THE UoNSDA.

UoNSDA Main Church is one of the nine churches that make up the University of Nairobi SDA Church. It is a dynamic community with approximately 180 active members. The church is organized into four ministries—Ark, Friends of Jesus, Pilgrims, and Vineyard—each led by 2 ministry chairs. Leadership is entrusted to three elders and a committed church board, ensuring smooth operations and spiritual growth. The church also hosts various weekly activities, including choir practice, Vespers, and Sabbath fellowship, fostering a strong sense of worship and fellowship. It also has a beautiful Choir, Advent Harmony which meets Every Wednesday and Friday.
